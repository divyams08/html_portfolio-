The Butterfly Effect is a 2004 psychological thriller that delves into the chaotic and unpredictable nature of time travel. Directed by Eric Bress and J. Mackye Gruber, the film stars Ashton Kutcher as Evan Treborn, a young man who discovers he can travel back in time and alter the course of his life by reading his childhood journals.
